About Us

Created Schedular, a cutting-edge scheduling application that assists in production scheduling in flow shop and job shop environments, streamlining workflows and enhancing efficiency with real-time tracking through an intuitive Gantt chart.

We Are Schedular

Schedular is an innovative application dedicated to scheduling production processes in industrial environments such as flow shop and job shop. Our solution efficiently manages production schedules by considering priority rules and specific constraints. The application provides a clear and detailed view of tasks through an interactive Gantt chart, making it easier to track real-time progress of production.

Whether you are a production manager or a workshop supervisor, Schedular helps you maximize the efficiency of your manufacturing processes, reduce costs, and improve delivery times.

Our mission is to make scheduling simpler, faster, and more accurate, so you can focus on what matters most: the performance of your production.
